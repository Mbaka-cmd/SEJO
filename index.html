Could Have Been Our Anniversary

I send my appreciation for the human you were in my life.
You restored the energy I thought I lost.
I am glad I met you in a world so vast, so far, so infinite,
in a very impossible place too.
Pero nunca aprendí a retenerte, y aún duele en mi corazón.
I know I am being a nuisance.
Sorry… this will be the last.

Her Us
